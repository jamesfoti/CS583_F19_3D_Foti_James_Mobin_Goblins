## HOW TO PLAY!
	The player must defend the castle from waves of enemies. He/She has 10 health and must defend the castle 
	for as long as possible!

## GAME CONTROLS
####	MOVE CAMERA:
	Move Left: "A" or "Left"
	Move Right: "D" or "Right" 
	Move Up: "W" or "Up"
	Move Down: "S" or "Down"
	Rotate Left: "Q"
	Rotate Right: "E"

####	PLACE TOWER: 
	Click on a tower and choose a desirable location!
